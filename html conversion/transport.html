Transport Facility

Bus Pass Assistance for Students

Overview

Government Polytechnic Dahod ensures convenient transport access by providing eligible students with official Bus Passes every semester. This facility aims to support daily commuting students from nearby areas.

The main bus station and railway station is located approximately 1 km from the college campus, making travel accessible and affordable for all enrolled students.

Bus Pass Renewal

  • Students must renew their bus pass every semester.
  • Renewal usually opens at the start of the academic term.
  • Valid College ID and enrollment documents are required.

Required Documents

  • College ID Card
  • Latest Fee Receipt
  • Aadhar Card (Photocopy)
  • Passport-sized Photograph
Note: Bus Pass Form and document checklist are available under the Student Corner section.
