Computer Department

🎯 Vision

To Prepare competent rural youth by catering soft skills and technical expertise in computer field, through conductive learning environment.

🚀 Mission

1. To implant professional attitute, moral values, technical expertise and innovative capabilities.
2. To prepare skilled computer engineers for specialized careers along with higher studies.
3. To enhance liaison with industry and academia for maximum exchange of knowledge and skill.
