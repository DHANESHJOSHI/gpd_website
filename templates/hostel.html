🏨 Hostel Facilities

Government Polytechnic Dahod provides well-maintained hostel facilities for both boys and girls. Our hostels ensure a safe, secure, and comfortable environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

🏢 Boys Hostel
  • 📌 Rooms: 40
  • 👨‍🎓 Capacity: 120 students
  • 🏠 Facilities: Common room, mess, purified drinking water
  • 🔒 Security: 24x7 surveillance and warden supervision
🏢 Girls Hostel
  • 🏫 Old Hostel Rooms: 16
  • 🏫 New Hostel Rooms: 20
  • 👩‍🎓 Total Capacity: 108 students
  • 🌟 Facilities: Separate mess, reading hall, clean sanitation
